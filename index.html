Broadly, my research is in the design and implementation of scalable, efficient, and resilient networked systems. Currently, I am working on: - **Datacenter transport offload**: Flexible, high-performance transport protocol offload to programmable network accelerators. Previously, I was a student researcher in the [Systems Research Group (SRG) at Google](https://cloud.google.com/blog/topics/systems/google-creates-new-systems-research-group) advised by [Dr. Kimberly Keeton](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=wR_tv-kAAAAJ&hl=en), where I studied: - **Tiered memory management**: Understanding the impact of swapping on application performance at datacenter scale. - **Representative benchmarks**: Methodology to synthesize memory tiering benchmarks representative of fleet behavior.
