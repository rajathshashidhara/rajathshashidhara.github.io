Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering University of Washington rajaths@cs.washington.edu [CV](cv.pdf) • [GitHub](https://github.com/rajathshashidhara) • [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rajath-s) • [Twitter](https://twitter.com/rajaths_) • [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/user/show/17609812-rajath-shashidhara) I am a Ph.D. student in the [UW Computer Systems Lab](https://syslab.cs.washington.edu/) advised by Prof. [Simon Peter](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~simpeter/). Broadly, my research is in the design and implementation of scalable, efficient and resilient networked systems. Presently, I am working on: - **Datacenter transport**: Flexible high-performance transport protocol offload to programmable network devices. - **Co-designing distributed systems with programmable networks**: In-network coordination and replica discovery in distributed filesystems. - **Client-local NVM filesystems**: Mechanisms for scaling client-local NVM distributed filesystems to datacenter scale.
